Who I Am

I'm a person who loves to learn and be creative, but since I can't draw to save my life, dived into the world of software development to use my creativity to solve logic puzzles. I love being in the tech world and seeing the cutting edge of the future, while getting to work on the problems of today. When I'm not grinding the keyboard, I'm passionate about traveling to see the world, supporting my Muay Thai gym & team, seeing live music, and tending to my wildflower garden.

What I've Done

I possess an Associate's Degree in Computer Science from Brown College, where I studied everything from software engineering to corporate network development, gaining hands-on experience with all forms of IT professional needs. I also have my Full Stack Software Engineering Certification from Prime Digital Academy, a deep-immersion full-time program, where I built full CRUD web applications with the latest languages while being entirely remote.

In my previous roles, I've served as an Executive Assistant for the largest energy-efficiency company in Minnesota, where I worked hand-in-hand with all executive staff to help manage the company. There I collaborated with the in-house software development team for quality assurance testing of all new features, providing detailed end-user feedback and troubleshooting.

Prior to that, I was an Assistant Manager for a kickboxing and fitness gym, where I coached class sizes of up to 30 students daily. I also directed private introductory lessons for prospective members, molding first impressions of the center and programs. It was there I gained my love of teaching, discovered my competitive side, while promoting physical wellbeing and self-defense knowledge.

What I've Created

Please feel free to look through some of my more recent projects. All of the code below are full-stack apps using CRUD methodology to have the front-end communicate with a virtual server. Feedback is always welcome!

Pack Me Up

Made for all the people who travel often and pack heavy, Pack Me Up aims to be the world's best packing app, and will be taking a suitcase of stress off of your mind by letting you organize everything you'd ever need to travel with into neat little boxes.

Snapshot Tiles

Snapshot Tiles is an app similar to Instagram or Facebook, where a user can upload their own pictures with descriptions, and viewers can click to "Like" the photo. When a photo is clicked, it flips to show the text with conditioning rendering.

The Movies Saga

Built to get hands-on experience with React, Redux, and Redux Saga, The Movies Saga app will let a user add, remove, edit, and update their movie library with names, descriptions, images, and categories.
